About Us

A story of tradition, passion, and authentic Indian flavors

Since 2005

Where Heritage Meets Modern Elegance

Spice & Elegance was born from a simple belief: that food is the most powerful expression of culture, memory, and love. Nestled in the heart of Bengaluru, our restaurant was founded by the Sharma family — who brought with them generations of culinary secrets passed down through the kitchens of Rajasthan and Punjab.

Every dish at our table is crafted with hand-picked spices, the freshest seasonal produce, and unwavering dedication to authentic flavors. We believe in the slow art of cooking — allowing ingredients to bloom and spices to develop their full depth, the way our grandmothers taught us.

Our warm, family-friendly dining room has been the backdrop for countless milestones — anniversary dinners, family celebrations, and quiet evenings of solace. We don't just serve food. We create memories.
